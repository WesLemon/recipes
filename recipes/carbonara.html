Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Discover how to make superb spaghetti carbonara. This cheesy pasta dish is an Italian favourite and with the right technique, you can make it perfect every time

This makes enough for one person. If you want to make it for two people, then double everything except for the pancetta and the egg white (i.e., instead of using 1 whole egg and one egg yolk, use one whole egg and two egg yolks)

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of pancetta
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 30g Pecorino Romano
  • 30g Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 1 serving of spaghetti
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 1/4 cup of starchy water (from cooking the spaghetti)

Steps

  1. Combine egg and egg yolk in a large bowl
  2. Grate the Pecorino and the Parmigiano and beat together with the eggs until homogenous, then season the mixture with salt and pepper
  3. Add the pancetta to a cold pan and then turn the heat to medium
  4. Allow to cook until it's done but not too crispy, turn off the heat but leave the pan where it is
  5. Cook the spaghetti to the desired texture and then mix everything together rigorously in the pan used to cook the pancetta
  6. Add the reserved pasta water to make the sauce creamier
  7. Enjoy straight away
